## SEO Tactics
- Every blog post targets 1 primary + 2 secondary keywords
- All pages have unique meta titles and descriptions
- Internal linking between blog posts and product pages
- Hreflang tags for no/en versions
- Structured data (JSON-LD) on blog posts and FAQ
- Canonical URLs on all pages
